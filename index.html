About Us

Resto Eatery's raw, plant-based menu is broad, delicious and diverse, perfect for anyone and everyone seeking the essential whole food energy that helps us THRIVE. All of Resto's menu items are made from hand-selected, high-quality, organic vegan ingredients to always achieve optimum freshness and flavour.

As we grow and evolve, Resto will continually strive to accommodate our valued customers’ needs, their tastes and their dietary requirements.
